**Unemployment Analysis in India Using Python** 🇮🇳

🧠 **Project Overview**

This project focuses on examining unemployment data from different regions of India to identify trends, regional differences, and the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic. Through Python-based data analysis and visualization, meaningful insights were generated to better understand employment patterns and economic changes.

To enhance user interaction, a Streamlit dashboard was also developed for dynamic exploration of the analysis results.

🎯 **Project Goals**

* Study unemployment rates across various regions of India.
* Prepare and clean the dataset to ensure reliable analysis.
* Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover key trends.
* Evaluate the impact of COVID-19 on unemployment levels.
* Detect regional and seasonal variations in employment patterns.

🛠️ **Tools & Technologies**

* Python
* Pandas – Data manipulation and analysis
* NumPy – Numerical operations
* Matplotlib & Seaborn – Data visualization and EDA
* Plotly – Interactive charts and graphs
* Streamlit – Interactive web dashboard development

📂 **Dataset Information**

**Source:** Unemployment in India Dataset (Kaggle)

**Dataset File:** `unemployment_data.csv`

The dataset contains:

* **Date** – Recorded observation date
* **Region** – State or region within India
* **Area** – Urban or Rural classification
* **Estimated Unemployment Rate (%)**
* **Estimated Employed Population**
* **Estimated Labour Participation Rate (%)**

🔍 **Data Preparation & Exploration**

### Data Preprocessing

* Handled missing and inconsistent records.
* Converted date-related fields into datetime format.
* Standardized and renamed columns for improved readability.

### Exploratory Data Analysis

* Generated descriptive statistics for employment-related indicators.
* Examined unemployment trends over time.
* Compared unemployment across regions and area types.
* Segmented data into pre-pandemic and pandemic periods for comparison.

📈 **Key Visualizations & Analysis**

✅ **Average Unemployment Rate by Region**

* Compared unemployment levels across different regions using bar charts.

✅ **Monthly Unemployment Trends**

* Visualized changes in unemployment rates over time through line graphs.

✅ **Regional Changes During COVID-19**

* Analyzed increases and decreases in unemployment across regions during the pandemic period.

✅ **Distribution of Unemployment Rates**

* Used histograms and density plots to understand unemployment patterns nationwide.

✅ **Labour Participation vs. Unemployment**

* Investigated the relationship between labour participation and unemployment through scatter plots.

✅ **Pre-COVID vs. During-COVID Analysis**

* Compared unemployment indicators before and during the pandemic to measure its impact.

📊 **Interactive Streamlit Dashboard**

A user-friendly Streamlit dashboard was created to make the analysis more accessible and interactive. Key features include:

* Region-based filtering options
* Urban and Rural area selection
* Interactive Plotly visualizations
* Easy navigation through different analytical insights and charts

💡 **Major Findings**

* The COVID-19 pandemic led to a noticeable increase in unemployment rates across India.
* Recovery rates differed significantly between regions.
* Certain areas showed stronger resilience and faster employment recovery.
* Seasonal employment trends revealed recurring fluctuations influenced by economic conditions.
